Campus Connect

Connecting you to the vibrant campus life!

Your one-stop hub for clubs and events

Organize, promote, and participate with ease!

Features for Organizers:

  • Create and manage events (name, date, time, location, description, agenda)
  • Set event visibility (public/private)
  • Manage capacity limits and registration deadlines
  • Track RSVPs and attendance
  • Promote events through social media and email
  • Facilitate communication with attendees (chat, forums)
  • Collect feedback and analyze event data
  • Share resources and collaborate with members

Benefits for Attendees:

  • Discover events from various clubs and organizations
  • Register for events and receive updates
  • Engage in discussions and provide feedback
  • Sync events with personal calendars
  • Access resources and collaborate with other members
